# Loops Marketplace: Terms of Service & Escrow Agreement

Welcome to Loops. By using our platform to buy or sell goods and services, you agree to the following terms, specifically regarding our Escrow Handshake system.

## 1. The Escrow Handshake
Loops uses an escrow-based payment system to ensure safety for both buyers and sellers on campus.
- **Payment**: When a buyer pays for an item, the funds are held securely by Loops.
- **Verification**: Funds are only released to the seller once the buyer and seller meet in person and a verification QR code (or token) is scanned.
- **Acceptance**: By scanning the handshake QR code, the buyer confirms they have inspected the item and are satisfied with its condition. **Once scanned, the transaction is non-refundable.**

## 2. Campus Safety Guidelines
- **Public Meetings**: Always meet in well-lit, public areas on campus (e.g., student centers, libraries, main plazas).
- **Inspection**: Buyers must thoroughly inspect items before scanning the handshake code.
- **No-Show**: If a party fails to show up for a meeting, the transaction can be disputed.

## 3. Disputes & Refunds
- **Opening a Dispute**: If an item is not as described OR if a meeting fails to occur, users must open a dispute **BEFORE** the handshake code is scanned.
- **Admin Review**: Loops administrators will review disputes. Evidence (chat history, photos) may be required.
- **Resolution**: Admins have the final say in releasing funds to the seller or refunding the buyer.

## 4. Platform Fees
- Loops charges a small platform fee (typically 5%) to maintain the service and secure escrow. This fee is non-refundable once a transaction is successfully completed.
