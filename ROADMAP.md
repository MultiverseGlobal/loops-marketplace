## 📦 Project Summaries

### 🔗 Loops
A premium student marketplace focused on seamless transactions between student vendors and buyers.
- **Web App:** Next.js + Supabase + Paystack/Escrow.
- **Mobile App:** React Native + Expo.
- **Goal:** Drive volume and trust through secure payments.

### 🏛️ Quad
The dedicated campus networking and opportunity platform.
- **Core Features:** Campus verification, opportunity boards, student feeds.
- **Goal:** The central hub for student life and career growth.
